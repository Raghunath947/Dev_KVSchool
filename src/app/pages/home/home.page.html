Welcome to IIT Bombay KV School Parents-Teachers Discussion Forum

This forum is built to facilitate better personalized communication between parents and teachers of children studying in IIT Bombay KV School. The short meeting times between parents and teachers when dropping children to school or when picking them up may not be conducive for more detailed discussions.

This forum hopes to address this by allowing parents and teachers to communicate at times of their convenience, while keeping the conversation personalized. It can also be used to provide constructive suggestions from parents to the school authorities and vice versa.
